2. Spans

This paragraph shows off all the cool things you can do with a span. You can make text bold or italic (or both). Text can be red, or blue. Maybe you want an underline. If you want to, you can put some space between certain text and the rest of the paragraph using padding or margin. You can even give some text a border. Maybe you love serif fonts? Or maybe you want some unusually large text in the middle of the paragraph for some reason? Or small text? The magic of spans never ends!

35th Annual
Apple Harvest Festival

Ithaca's premier event of the Fall, Apple Harvest Festival is a three-day celebration of food, rides, games, more than 100 vendors and, of course, APPLES! This spectacular celebration also kicks off Finger Lakes Cider Week 2017!

Friday, Sept 29 - 12pm-6pm

Saturday, Sept 30 - 10am-6pm

Sunday, Oct 1 - 10am-6pm

The event locates in downtown Ithaca, Ithaca, NY, 14850.
